# About J. PENIS 格调

**J. PENIS 格调**  
*(Journal of Proceedings of Engineering and Natural Information Systems)*  
is an independent, non-commercial, experimental online publication.

The project adopts the external structure and tone of academic proceedings while intentionally allowing unconventional content, incomplete studies, speculative ideas, and stylistic parody.

---

## Purpose

The purpose of J. PENIS 格调 is to:

- Experiment with academic formality as a creative medium  
- Explore the boundaries between serious scholarship and structured nonsense  
- Provide a low-pressure space for exploratory, humorous, or unfinished ideas  
- Parody conventional academic publishing without impersonating real journals  

---

## What This Journal Is

- Online-only  
- Open-access  
- Non-profit  
- Irregularly published  
- Structurally academic in appearance  

---

## What This Journal Is Not

- Not a peer-recognized academic journal  
- Not intended for academic credit, evaluation, or citation  
- Not affiliated with any university, institution, or publisher  

---

## Language

Articles may be published in English or Chinese.

---

## Transparency Statement

This journal is explicitly a **parody and experimental project**.  
Any resemblance to formal academic publishing is intentional in form, but not in function.
